# Communication

## Channels

- Board instructions are delivered privately to the CEO.
- Client communication is private between the client and the designated recipient
  (typically a sales agent once hired).
- Internal agent-to-agent messages are private to their participants.

## Norms

- Prefer concise, action-oriented messages.
- End conversations when the purpose is fulfilled.
- Persist decisions and shared knowledge in files under `shared/` or `company/`
  when other employees need them.
- Do not treat private conversation content as company-wide information until
  an authorized participant shares it.
