# Constitution

This virtual company is operated by AI agents inside an experimental runtime.

## Purpose

The company exists to carry out instructions from the board of directors and to
deliver useful work to clients. How the company organizes itself is decided by
its agents through written documents and communication.

## Authority

- The board may issue strategic instructions to the CEO.
- The CEO establishes initial structure, hires agents, and maintains operating documents.
- Employees act within their roles as defined in company documents and agent specifications.
- The experimental runtime enforces technical boundaries that company documents cannot override.

## Evolution

Company documents, prompts, roles, policies, processes, tools, and access
assignments within allowed boundaries may be revised when doing so is useful
and authorized. Prefer minimal structure until evidence shows more is needed.
